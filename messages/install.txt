Slatewave
=========

Thanks for installing Slatewave! Slate below, teal above.

To activate:

  1. Preferences → Select Color Scheme… → "Slatewave"
  2. Preferences → Select Theme…        → "Slatewave"

Or paste this into your user settings (Preferences → Settings):

    "color_scheme": "Packages/Slatewave/Slatewave.sublime-color-scheme",
    "theme": "Slatewave.sublime-theme"

Palette, syntax mapping, and companion themes (VSCode, Obsidian,
Oh My Posh, and more) are documented at:

    https://getslatewave.com/

Issues and PRs welcome at:

    https://github.com/kevinlangleyjr/sublime-text-slatewave
